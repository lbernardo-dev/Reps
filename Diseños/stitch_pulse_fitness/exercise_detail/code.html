Barbell Bench Press

Equipment

  • Flat Bench

Instructions

1

Lie flat on your back on the bench. Grip the bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.

2

Brace your core and press your feet flat into the ground. Lift the bar off the rack and position it above your chest with arms fully extended.

3

Inhale and slowly lower the bar to the middle of your chest, keeping your elbows at about a 45-degree angle to your body.

4

Exhale as you press the bar back to the starting position explosively. Pause briefly before beginning the next repetition.
